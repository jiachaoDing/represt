# Google Play Assets

## App Name
TrainRe

## Short Description
Track strength training plans, sets, and rest reminders.

## Full Description
TrainRe is a lightweight training log for strength workouts. Plan today's training, manage exercise templates, record completed sets, and get reminded when rest time ends.

Core features:
- Today's training plan and exercise ordering
- Template management and import
- Set, weight, and rep recording
- Training summaries and calendar history
- Local rest reminders and haptic feedback

## Data Safety Positioning
Training records, templates, and settings are stored primarily on the device. The app does not require account sign-in and does not proactively upload training data.

## Notification / Exact Reminder Permission Explanation
Notifications remind you to continue training when a rest countdown ends. Exact reminder permission improves rest timer punctuality.
